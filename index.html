Frequently asked questions

Is Meal Curate free to use?
Yes! Meal planning, recipe management, pantry tracking, and shopping lists are all completely free. Premium features come with a 14-day free trial so you can try everything before committing. After the trial, Premium is just $1.99/month for the full nutrition dashboard, macro tracking, trend analysis, and health tips.
What data does Meal Curate collect?
Meal Curate stores your data locally on your device using Core Data. We do not collect personal information or share your data with third parties. If you use the recipe recommendation feature, anonymous queries are sent to the Spoonacular API. See our Privacy Policy for full details.
Does the app work offline?
Yes. All your meal plans, recipes, pantry items, and shopping lists are stored on-device and work without an internet connection. An internet connection is only needed for AI recipe recommendations and recipe imports from the web.
Can I sync across devices?
Meal Curate supports iCloud Sync, which can be enabled in Settings. By default, data is stored locally for maximum reliability and privacy.
How do I cancel my subscription?
You can manage or cancel your Premium subscription at any time through your iPhone's Settings > Apple ID > Subscriptions. You'll retain Premium access until the end of your current billing period.
Which iOS versions are supported?
Meal Curate requires iOS 16.0 or later and is compatible with iPhone. The app is built with SwiftUI for a native, fast experience.
