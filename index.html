Questions.

Do I need an account?

No. Open the app and start. Your data stays on your device. There's no cloud login, no signup form, no email verification.

Does it work offline?

Yes. The 524 starter recipes are bundled with the app. Pantry, meal plan, shopping list — all local. The only thing that needs internet is optional URL recipe import.

What's the free tier?

Browse all 524 recipes. Build a meal plan. Track your pantry. Use the auto-generated shopping list. Save up to 5 of your own recipes. Pro adds pantry-aware ranking, unlimited recipes, URL import, and shopping list sharing.

How does it compare to Mealime, Paprika, Plan to Eat?

Mealime requires an account before showing a recipe. Paprika is excellent but doesn't know what's in your pantry. Plan to Eat is $39/year and built for power users. Meal Curate is for the cook who wants to open the app and see what they can make tonight, without signing up for anything.

Will there be Android, iPad, or Mac versions?

iPad and Mac launch with iOS — same app, universal binary. Android is not on the roadmap.
